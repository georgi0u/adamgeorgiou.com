Hormones Vocab

  • Leptin - Controls a number of metabolic processes. High the better.
  • Insulin - Slows down the processing of fatty acids. The lower the better.
  • Ghrelin - Hunger hormone. Tells you when it's time to eat. Train the timing of this.
  • Cortisol - Stress hormone. Produced from huge calorie deficits and extreme workouts. Associated with belly fat storage.

Prescribed Plan

Don't maintain extreme calorie deficits. Eat to fuel your workout, and still be in the negative. Maintain a deficit but keep it reasonable.

Plan cheat meals to keep leptin high.

Cyclical carbohydrate fasting.
